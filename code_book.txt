Code book
=================

subject : The subject identification from 1 to 30
variable : The variables for which mean is calculated, possible values are mean_x, mean_y, mean_z, std_x, std_y, std_z
mean_x : mean of x acceleration
mean_y : mean of y acceleration
mean_z : mean of z acceleration
std_x : standard deviation of x acceleration
std_y : standard deviation of y acceleration
std_z : standard deviation of z acceleration
